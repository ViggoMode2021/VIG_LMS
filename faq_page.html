  • General (This page is under construction!)

  • What is VigLMS?

    VigLMS is an LMS (Learning Management System) created by former high school Spanish teacher and current backend software developer, Ryan Viglione. VigLMS has capabilities akin to popular LMS software like PowerSchool and Google Classroom.

  • What technology is used by VigLMS?

    VigLMS is coded in Python/Flask (backend) and simple HTML and CSS (frontend). This faq page is the only page on the app that has Javascript. We use a PostgreSQL database powered by Amazon RDS that is connected with Psycopg2. For file upload and download, we use an Amazon S3 bucket that is connected with Boto3. VigLMS is deployed on Google Cloud App Engine and the domain is powered by Google as well.

  • Teacher Account

  • How do I sign up?

    Teachers can create an account for free by clicking on the 'No Account? Register' link on the front 'Teacher login page'. The form will prompt you to fill out: your name, your email, your username (create one), your password (create one), your class name, and your answer to the secret question: 'What is the name of the street you grew up on?'. The secret question is used to reset your password. VigLMS is still in production on a daily basis, so we hope to change this to a more modern, email password reset system.

  • How do I reset my password?

    You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot password? Reset password' link on the front 'Teacher login' page. You can reset your password by submitting: your email, your new password, and the answer to the secret question: 'What is the name of the street you grew up on?'.

  • How do I delete my account?

    You can delete your teacher account by filling out the 'delete teacher account' form on the front 'Teacher login page. You need to submit your email, username, and password.

  • How do I enroll or remove a student?

    You can enroll a student by clicking the 'Enroll New Student' on the 'class roster' page. In the fields of the form, type: first name, last name, graduation year, grade (0-100), and student email. The student's information will appear in table on the 'class roster' page. You can remove a student by typing their first and last name in the 'First name' and 'Last name' boxes respectively.

    Can I edit a student's information?

    You can edit a student's information by clicking on the 'Student First Name', 'Student Last Name', and 'Graduation Year' columns. This will open a page when the student's first name, last name, and graduation year can be updated. You cannot update the student's email address once it has been submitted upon enrollment.

    Can I edit a student's grade?

    You can edit a student's grade a few different ways. The most direct way is to override the student's grade by typing in the updated grade into the update grade fields of the table on the 'class roster' page. Also, whenever assignment grades are submitted, the student's grade changes to the average of all of their assignment grades.

    How can I add and remove an assignment from my grade book?

    Assignments are added to the grade book by clicking on the 'Add New Assignment' button on the 'View my assignments' page. This button will open a modal where you can input: assignment name, category, due date, and overall points. The assignment will appear in the table that appears on the assignment page. Assignments can be deleted by typing the name of the assignment in the 'Assignment name' box on the assignment page and clicking the 'Delete assignment' button.

    How can I add student grades for an assignment?

    Assignment grades for students are added by clicking on the 'Add/update student grades' button in the table on the 'view my assignments' page. You will input the necessary fields next to each student. Remember that VigLMS will then average the scores of all of the student's assignment grades and update the students overall grade.

    How can I add update student grades for an assignment?

    Assignment grades for students are updated by clicking on the 'ID' link or the 'Grade' link in the table on the 'class roster' page. You will input the necessary fields next to each student's grade for the assignment desired. Remember that VigLMS will then average the scores of all of the student's assignment grades and update the students overall grade.

    How can I update student information?

    Student information is updated by clicking on either the 'student first name', 'student last name', or 'graduation year' links in the table on the 'class roster page'. From there, you may update either the student's first name, last name, or graduation year.

    How can I take attendance?

    Attendance can be taken by clicking on the 'take attendance' tab of the navbar. In the table on the 'take attendance' page, you may mark students as 'present', 'tardy', or 'absent'.

    How can I search attendance by date or student?

    Attendance can be search by clicking on the 'take attendance' tab of the navbar. You can search attendance by date in the 'Search attendance by date' box at the bottom. You can search attendance by student by typing the student's id in the 'Search attendance by student' box.

    How can I post announcements to the student portal?

    Announcements can be posted to the student portal by typing the announcement in the box and clicking the 'Post announcement for today' button. These announcements can be seen by all students in the class when they log into the student portal.

    How can I send messages to students?

    Announcements can be posted to the student portal by typing the announcement in the box and clicking the 'Post announcement for today' button.

    How can I send messages to students?

    Students can be sent a message by clicking on the 'Send direct message' button on the 'class roster' page in the table next to their name. Students will see this when they log in to the student portal and teachers may see the messages by clicking the 'View direct messages (outbox) button in the table next to their name.

    How can I view messages from students?

    Messages sent by a particular student can be viewed by clicking on "View direct messages (Inbox)" on the 'class roster' page in the table next to their name. These messages are sent via the student's account in the student portal.

    How can I upload files to be accessed on the student portal?

    Files can be uploaded on the "upload files" page. These files can be things like: notes, assignments, slideshows, videos music etc. For example, a teacher uploads a file called 'Worksheet regular verbs' as PDF document to their portal and the student portal. The student on the student portal can download that file, edit it, and upload it from their end to be accessed by the teacher.
