  • General

  • What is VigLMS?

    VigLMS is an LMS (Learning Management System) created by former high school Spanish teacher and current backend software developer, Ryan Viglione. VigLMS has capabilities akin to popular LMS software like PowerSchool and Google Classroom.

  • What technology is used by VigLMS?

    VigLMS is coded in Python/Flask (backend) and simple HTML and CSS (frontend). This faq page is the only page on the app that has Javascript. We use a PostgreSQL database powered by Amazon RDS that is connected with Psycopg2. For file upload and download, we use an Amazon S3 bucket that is connected with Boto3. VigLMS is deployed on Google Cloud App Engine and the domain is powered by Google as well.

  • Teacher Account

  • How do I sign up?

    Teachers can create an account for free by clicking on the 'No Account? Register' link on the front login page. The form will prompt you to fill out: your name, your email, your username (create one), your password (create one), your class name, and your answer to the secret question: 'What is the name of the street you grew up on?'. The secret question is used to reset your password. VigLMS is still in production on a daily basis, so we hope to change this to a more modern, email password reset system.

  • How do I reset my password?

    You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot password? Reset password' link on the front login page. You can reset your password by submitting: your email, your new password, and the answer to the secret question: 'What is the name of the street you grew up on?'.

  • How do I delete my account?

    You can delete your teacher account by filling out the 'delete teacher account' form on the teacher login page. You need to submit your email, username, and password.
